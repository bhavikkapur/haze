Why HAZE

Measurement is not accountability.

Existing air quality monitors are good at telling us how polluted the air is. They are rarely good at telling us where that pollution actually came from.

HAZE exists to explore whether low-cost hardware, careful sensing and intelligent analysis can move the conversation from measuring pollution to identifying its sources — publicly, honestly, and in the open.
